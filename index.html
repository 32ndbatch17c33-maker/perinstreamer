The Creator's Note

Hi, my name is John Carlo Perin. Today is June 26, 2026, and it is currently the midyear season at my school, UPLB. I am working as a student assistant at our college, but I still find myself with a lot of free time—and frankly, I’ve been bored. Over the past few weeks, I watched so many shows and movies that I think I have finally run out of things to watch!

Yesterday, I decided to create something interesting. Because I am very product-oriented, I wanted to do something creative and push myself past mediocrity. So, I built a streaming app!

Since video hosting is difficult to find and incredibly expensive, and because I don't own original content, I utilized YouTube videos since they allow legal embedding. I am just really happy that I managed to build something meaningful before going to sleep.

There are so many amazing tools in our world today, and I am grateful to live in this era. Moving forward, I hope to remain curious, creative, and grounded.
